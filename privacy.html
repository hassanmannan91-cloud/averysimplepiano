Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

Thank you for using A Very Simple Piano. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information our app collects and how it is used.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior outside of the app.
  • We do not serve ads or share any data with third parties.

Information the App May Use

  • When you use the built-in recorder, recordings are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Settings (such as Scale Mode, Octave, or Reverb) are saved locally on your device to improve your experience.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, or anyone at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
